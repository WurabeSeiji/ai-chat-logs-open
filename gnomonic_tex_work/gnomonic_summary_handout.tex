\noindent\textbf{Overview:}
Starting from a single geometric construction --- the gnomonic (central) projection onto a hypersphere --- we derive the Einstein equation with cosmological constant, the Lorentzian signature, charge quantization, the Coulomb $1/r^2$ law, and the complete Maxwell equations. The only free parameter is a single coupling constant $C$ with the dimension of length.

\subsection*{Paper 1: Geometric Formulation of 4D Spacetime}
\vspace{-6pt}
\begin{itemize}
\item Maps 4D Euclidean space onto $S^4(R)$ via gnomonic projection
\item Derives the pullback metric $g_{\mu\nu}$ and the Einstein tensor
\item \textbf{Main result:} $G_{\mu\nu} + \Lambda\, g_{\mu\nu} = 0$ with $\Lambda = 3/R^2$
\item The cosmological constant is \emph{geometrically determined} by the curvature radius $R$
\item Unified notation for Euclidean/Lorentzian signatures via $\epsilon_\mu \in \{+1,-1\}$
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Paper 2: Geometric Origin of Lorentzian Spacetime}
\vspace{-6pt}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Axiom A:} The $R$-direction of $S^n(R)$ is unobservable (Gauss's Theorema Egregium)
\item \textbf{Axiom B:} Unobservable dimensions undergo imaginary rotation ($R \to iR$)
\item These axioms yield the Lorentzian signature $(-,+,+,+)$ from pure geometry
\item Introduces the \textbf{sole coupling constant} $C$ (dimension: length, $C \sim \ell_P$)
\item Discrete gnomonic mapping on integer lattice $\mathbb{Z}^{1+N}$
\item \textbf{Arithmetic anomaly:} Isotropic null geodesics impossible for $N=3$, possible for $N=4$
\item $\Longrightarrow$ Discrete spacetime \textbf{requires a 5th dimension}
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Paper 3: Charge Dimension and Electromagnetic Field}
\vspace{-6pt}
\begin{itemize}
\item Identifies the 5th dimension with the \textbf{charge (Coulomb) dimension} $\phi = Qq$
\item Conversion coefficient $Q = C/e_0$ ($e_0 = e/3$: quark charge quantum)
\item \textbf{Charge quantization} $q = n e_0$ from the discrete lattice (no compactification)
\item Derives the \textbf{$1/r^2$ Coulomb law} from gnomonic curvature in the $\phi$-direction
\item Combined with Lorentz invariance $\Longrightarrow$ \textbf{all four Maxwell equations}
\item Equivalence principle: consequence of unobservability of $R$; absent for EM because $\phi$ is observable
\item Spin-1 (EM) vs.\ spin-2 (gravity) from directed vs.\ undirected curvature
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{What This Theory Does \underline{Not} Claim}

\vspace{-2pt}
\begin{itemize}
\item Does not replace General Relativity --- derives vacuum Einstein equations as geometric identity
\item Does not predict the value of $e_0$ --- it is experimentally determined ($e_0 = e/3$ from quarks)
\item Does not derive the fine-structure constant $\alpha \approx 1/137$ (future task)
\item All results are confined to pure geometry and number theory
\end{itemize}
